Introducing the IV-21
The pen perfected
Writing. Timekeeping.
Data Storage. Security. Flashlight. All in one.
Swiss made Cold War tested

ROTATE

Powered by Cold War era Soviet IV-21 vacum florescent display tubes, each Swiss made Gelfman IV-21 pen uses the same tube technology that powered MiG aircraft radar. "Gelfman: where the past meets the future." Gelfman: where the past meets the future.

Due to limited IV-21 VFD supplies, only 6,000

IV-21 pens will ever be made

  • Vacuum Fluorescent
    Display

    The IV-21 VFD tube was used by the Soviet Union to power a variety of Cold War era technology. Discontinued in 1990, Gelfman acquired the world’s remaining IV-21 VFD tubes. Each Gelfman IV-21 writing instrument is powered inside by a Soviet VFD tube from the Cold War. VFD tubes are prized for their vivid, powerful light. With only a finite amount of IV-21 VFD tubes left in the world, Gelfman crafted a true limited-edition.

  • Data Stored Data Secure

    Ultra reliable USB memory chip with hardware encryption completely protects data. Users can erase all saved data instantly with just one simple click.
