repair & restoration

A fine mechanical watch can be passed from one generation to another and its value would still increase. But like a luxury car, it needs regular maintenance and professional care. Normally a mechanical watch requires to be serviced every 3-5 years – this will help your watch last literally forever.

Your valued timepiece deserves the best care possible, whether it comes to a case polishing or to complete overhaul. We have been caring for fine watches from 1973.

Our watchmakers specialize in A.Lange & Söhne, Patek Philippe and Vacheron Constantin, apart from that we repair over 100 luxury watch brands, including Breitling, Rolex, Cartier, Omega and Tag Heuer.

Our expert Paul Riekmann, former chief watchmaker of the renowned Meister Cie on Bahnhofstrasse Zurich, or other fine craftsmen known to us, will service your timepiece with dedication and professional expertise.

Do not hesitate to contact us for a detailed work and price offer.
